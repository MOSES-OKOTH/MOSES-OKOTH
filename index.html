1. RH Radio

RH Radio is an online media hosting platform, mostly mixtapes. This was my first production project back in 2019. The website is owned and managed by Richy Haniels - a music promoter and music producer based in Nairobi, Kenya.
The site allows DJs across Kenya to host their video mixes in the platform allowing them to reach wider masses.
Plans have been there on monetization of the platform when it hits at least 100,000 monthly users. That is yet to be implemented so as to allow local DJs to earn from the platform through sharing their talents.

2. Dammy Twitch

dammytwitch.com is a website I made for a Nigerian video director, Apampa Owolabi Oluwadamilola - porpularly known as Dammy Twitch.
The website is meant to showcase his work as a video director who has worked on various hits in the Afro genre that has won various regional and international awards.
The site implements vimeo's API that allows the director to share the videos he has worked on in his site directly from Vimeo's website by just uploading the link in his administrator panel and the video is automatically shared in the site.
This is one of the most successful projects I have ever done because it tops at an average of 10,000 active monthly users and generates revenue to the director.
